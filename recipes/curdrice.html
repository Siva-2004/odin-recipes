Curd rice

Description

Curd rice, also called yogurt rice, is a dish originating from India. The word "curd" in Indian English refers to unsweetened probiotic yogurt.

It is most popular in the South Indian states of Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Ingirdients:

  • Rice.
  • Curd.
  • Carrot, Cucumber, Coriander leaves (optional).

Steps:

  1. Cook the Rice.
  2. Mash the rice slightly.
  3. Let the rice cool completely. Add curd to the cooled rice.
  4. Add little amount of salt.
  5. Stir in Coriander leaves, grated carrots and cucumbers to the curd to add some crunch and taste.
