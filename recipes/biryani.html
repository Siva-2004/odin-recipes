Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Description

Hyderabadi biryani is very simple to prepare and this recipe is perfect for home and it does not require sauteing of spices or meat.

Just marinate the meat with the spices and the ingridients. Layer partially cooked basmati rice over it. Cook on a slow dum process.

Ingridients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 6 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt(adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1 pinch saffron (optional)

Steps

  1. Take a bowl and add the masala items and the plain yogurt along with lemon juice and mix it well.
  2. Now add chicken to the mixture and mix well and let it marinate for a couple of hours.
  3. Meanwhile rinse and wash the rice thoroughly.
  4. Take a vessel and heat ghee or oil.
  5. Add whole spices like Bayleaf, cloves, cinnamon, star anise,cardamom, shahi jeera.
  6. When the spices begin to sizzle, add onions and fry them evenly stirring often until uniformly light brown.
  7. Add chicken and saute until pale for about 5 minutes on a medium heat.
  8. Cover and cook on alow flame until the chicken is soft cooked and tender. Saute to evaporate excess water.
  9. Taste test and add salt, slit green chilli, red chilli powder and garam masala.
  10. Mix everything well and layer chicken evenly at the bottom.
  11. Spread drained rice in a layer over the chicken.
  12. Add salt and mix. Taste the water. It must be slightly salty.
  13. Pour 2 cups of water across the sides of the vessel and let it rest on top of the rice.
  14. Sprinkle saffron soaked milk and mint leaves.
  15. Cover the pot and let the biryani cook on medium flame for about 20 minutes.
  16. Enjoy Chicken biryani with raita and sliced veggies.
